Our Heritage

The Heart of
Healthy Baking


Founded by Kaveya Karthik (BE Electronics & Communication Engineering, ME Applied Electronics), this home bakery started with a mother's mission — to give her twin daughters healthy and safe homemade treats.

What began as baking for family soon grew into a passion to serve the community with nutritious, preservative-free baked goods made from millets, cold-pressed oils, khandsari sugar, and high-quality cocoa.

Every product is baked fresh in small batches from our home kitchen in Manachanallur, with no shortcuts and no compromises.

Healthy. Homemade. Honest.

Kaveya Karthik

Founder & Baker · Assistant Professor, Engineering College

I am a mother of twin girls. My baking journey began when I wanted to prepare healthy snacks for my children — without preservatives, artificial ingredients, or refined products.

Experimenting with millets, natural sweeteners, and simple ingredients, I slowly created recipes that are both tasty and nutritious. Today, through Manni Twins Healthy Home Bakes, I share these homemade treats with families who want better, healthier baked options for their children and loved ones.
